In N Out Versus Five Guys:

When it comes to a good burger, the West Coast and East Coast always seem to argue about which is better. Is it the fast, iconic, and cheap burger from Southern California? Or the pricey, heavy, but filling burger from Arlington Virginia?

The Beauty of Stainglass Windows:

Ever wondered why they seem so important to a Church or Bascilica's structure? The tradition actually started all the way back into the fourth century. Not only did these beautiful pieces help tell biblical stories to the illiterate majority, but they also provide vibrant colors inside the interior.

Is Self Soothing a Myth?

The popular term "Self Soothing" was coined by Dr Thomas Anders in the 1970's. It seemed the right solution to infants suffering from colic, or possibly being "velcro" babies. However, due to advancements in child development and psychology we now know that it has no real scientific backing.
